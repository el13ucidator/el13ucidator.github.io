About el13ucidator

The name of this site refers not to the author. Rather, the name and purpose is derived from the Elucidator sword, a tool. Here, I intend to use writing to consolidate information, as well as to explore and share ideas. Like the series that engendered the Elucidator, a focus of this site will be the application of scientific knowledge and, sometimes, engineering's partnership with medicine.

About the author

I am currently an MD-PhD student researching the molecular basis of gene expression -- more specifically, how RNA is made from a DNA template and what happens when that process is deregulated. My work largely involves pipetting at the bench and sometimes coding to explore genomic or RNA sequencing data.
